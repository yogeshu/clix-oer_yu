English

Communicative English Level 1 has been designed for learners in the A1/A2 CEFR level. The module contains 20 hours of content that focuses on listening skills and comprehension. In the first 10 lessons, the focus is on building a theme based vocabulary. These lessons come with bilingual support in Hindi and Telugu for early language learners. As the lessons advance, the language input increases gradually from words and phrases to sentences. In the next 10 lessons, the English input gradually increases and the lessons are woven around simple stories.
